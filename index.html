Native Plants of San Marcos, TX

Exploring the unique flora of the Texas Hill Country and San Marcos River.

Texas Wild Rice (Zizania texana)

Type: Aquatic Grass

An endangered species found only in the upper San Marcos River. It forms long, streaming clumps in the clear, spring-fed water.

Texas Persimmon (Diospyros texana)

Type: Small Tree / Shrub

Known for its beautiful peeling grey bark and small, edible black fruits that are a favorite for local wildlife.

Texas Lantana (Lantana urticoides)

Type: Wildflower / Shrub

A hardy, drought-tolerant plant with vibrant orange and yellow flowers that attract butterflies across San Marcos.

Bald Cypress (Taxodium distichum)

Type: Large Tree

These iconic trees line the banks of the San Marcos River, recognizable by their "knees" that poke out of the water.
